About CattleGuard AI

8-class cattle skin disease detection using MobileNetV2 transfer learning.

Project Overview

CattleGuard AI is a deep learning-powered diagnostic web application that detects 8 cattle skin conditions from uploaded photographs. Built on MobileNetV2 transfer learning with TensorFlow/Keras, it provides instant predictions with confidence scores, making professional-grade screening accessible to farmers and field veterinarians without specialist equipment.
